Mysure Blog

Mysore, also known as Mysuru, is a vibrant city located in the southern Indian state of Karnataka. It is famous for its rich cultural heritage, stunning palaces, intricate architecture, and delicious cuisine. The city is also a hub for yoga and spirituality, attracting visitors from all over the world.

Banglore Blog

Bangalore, also known as Bengaluru, is a bustling city located in the southern Indian state of Karnataka. It is known as the Silicon Valley of India due to its thriving IT industry. The city is also famous for its pleasant weather, vibrant nightlife, delicious food, and rich cultural heritage.

Patna Blog

Patna is the capital city of the northern Indian state of Bihar. The city boasts of a rich historical and cultural legacy, with several ancient temples, mosques, and monuments that attract tourists from all over the world. The Mahavir Mandir, Patna Museum, and the Takht Sri Patna Sahib are some of the must-visit places in the city. Additionally, Patna is also famous for its delicious cuisine, including traditional Bihari dishes like litti-chokha and sattu-paratha.

Bhagalpur Blog

Bhagalpur is a city in the eastern Indian state of Bihar, situated on the banks of the Ganges river. The city is known for its lush greenery, scenic beauty, and rich cultural heritage. The Vikramshila Gangetic Dolphin Sanctuary, the Bhagalpur Silk City, and the Colganj Rock Cut Temples are some of the popular tourist attractions in the city.
